Minesweeper

Instructions

The board to your right contains hidden mines, signalized by an X. If you open a cell with a mine, you lose! If you open all cells without a mine, you win! The beginner level has 10 hidden mines. The intermediate has 25 mines.

A right-click will open the cell, and a left click will change the cell's background to red and decrease the mine counter by one.

Cells without a mine have a number that indicates the number of mines around them. Use these clues to win.

If you want to RESET the game, click the face on top of the board

Good Luck!
